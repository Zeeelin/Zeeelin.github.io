Wolverinesoft Studio Projects

Wolverinesoft Studio, based at the University of Michigan, operates with a dedicated team of up to 46 student members, encompassing roles such as producers, artists, programmers, audio designers, designers, and marketing professionals. Our studio thrives on producing and publishing one game per semester, making them available on platforms like itch.io and Steam.
Within this dynamic environment, I serve as the UI/UX and technical artist for Curse of the Corsair, while also contributing as a 2D artist and narrative designer for both Regnark TD and Subtention.

Curse of the Corsair: A top-down hack and slash game based on Hyper Light Drifter.
Game Description: Echoes of a swashbuckling and treacherous past reverberate through a vast and untamed sea, brimming with riches and danger. In this action-packed adventure RPG, you'll set sail on a journey inspired by the modern classic Hyper Light Drifter, but with a salty, scurvy twist.
https://store.steampowered.com/app/2365570/Curse_of_the_Corsair/

RegnarkTD: A tower defense game based on BloomTD.
Game Description: Ragnarök TD is a 2D top-down tower defense game created as a case study of Ninja Kiwi's Bloons TD 6 by the University of Michigan's WolverineSoft Studio. Follow the story of a small village in the North as they fend off the beasts of Ragnarök in this revamped Steam iteration of the original project.
https://store.steampowered.com/app/2365570/Curse_of_the_Corsair/

Subtension: A real time simulation(RTS) based on FTL faster than light.
Game Description: Outfit, crew and command your submarine to save what's left of humanity beneath the seas of a ruined earth. Man your sub's systems and take aim in this real-time strategy epic! Inspired by FTL, this subnautic student project serves as our first 2-semester release.
https://store.steampowered.com/app/2251660/Subtension/

EECS 498 Extended Reality and Society Final Project:: Make a Mind

Make a Mind utilizes Virtual Reality technology to present a variety of mini-games that aim to reduce stress, anxiety and promote relaxation. With personalized challenges, guided meditations and interactive experiences, the game offers a unique way to focus on your wellbeing.
For more about Make a Mind, please visit: https://yonghuan-hu.github.io/make-a-mind/index.html

In addition, I am currently engaged in an exciting endeavor for my honor thesis. Collaborating with esteemed professors from Michigan Medicine and computer science department, I am exploring the potential of virtual reality as a complementary tool to traditional exposure therapy. This research aims to delve into the possibilities and benefits of utilizing VR technology in therapeutic settings.

About me:

This unique blend of expertise empowers me to approach challenges from diverse perspectives, making me well-equipped to contribute effectively to projects and initiatives that require a synthesis of psychology and computer science.
