Description of the Project

Curse of the Corsair is a pirate-themed case study of Heart Machine's Hyper Light Drifter.
It was created by 40 students at the University of Michigan's WolverineSoft Studio.

Role in the Project

As a member of the art department at WolverineSoft Studio, I joined the User Experience
pod creating UI/UX art assets. I was responsible for designing intuitive interfaces influenced
by the minimalist approach in the original Hyper Light Drifter. In addition, I worked closely
with artists and programmers from the character design pod to create and rig character animation
for players and enemies. Some of the animation includes, player attack, enemy attack, player death etc.
