Description of the Project

Subtension was a submarine simulation roguelike-like case study of Subset Game's FTL:
Faster Than Light. It is a two-semester-long project prototyped by the Summer team at
WolverineSoft Studio and finished by the Fall team. A total of 40 students worked on this game.

Role in the Project

As a member of the art department at WolverineSoft Studio, I was in charge of the background
concept art and character animation for this game. I also volunteered in the narrative team to
create a multi-ending story of an apocalyptic world where people can only live underwater with
the shelter of either a city or submarines.
