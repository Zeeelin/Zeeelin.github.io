Beneath the Dream (Summer 2024)

Description of the Project

Beneath the Dream is a short 3-page comic preview I created on the adventure
of a homeless girl with her friend in the dream work. The protagonist, Louis,
is trying to investigate why there are increasing kidnapping cases within the area,
and she does so by entering the dream world with her friend Carlo.

Role in the Project

This is an individual comic project created during my time at Birdhouse Comics, and it is
included in Birdhouse Comics Vol.1 published in Summer 2024.
