Description of the Project

Pumpkin-Take Me Home is a 2d platformer game created during Fall 2021 by a team of 4.
It tells the story of a man who returns from death with a pumpkin as his head to visit his
family one last time.

Role in the Project

I was the only artist on this team, while the others were all programmers. The narrative and
concept were created during the initial discussion after we received the theme of this game jam.
The art and the animations were all done using Procreate. It is a game that resonates with me
personally. Not only is it the first game I ever helped to create, but also, while it is a game
about death, I experienced losing a close family member the next day this game was created. Despite
that, the family member who passed away was able to play this prior to his death, and he sense my passion
in game creation and encouraged me to continue making games. It is certainly one of my motivations to
continue on this journey of game development.
